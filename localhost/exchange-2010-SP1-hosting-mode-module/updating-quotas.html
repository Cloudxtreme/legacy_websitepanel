Updating Quotas

Translations:

  • tr-TR

Admin or Reseller will update quotas by clicking on Account Home – Hosting Plans and selecting Hosting Plan to edit. In the example below, admin updates quotas for ‘HostingSample,3’ hosting plan from 15 to 20 for domains, mailboxes, contacts and distribution lists:

After new quotas are saved, user will see the following quotas on their Exchange Hosting Mode screen:

Quotas till show limit of 15, but (max 20) indicates that user may increase the limit up to 20. User will click on Update Quotas button and see Update Organization Quotas screen:

User will change quotas to 20 for Mailboxes, Contacts and Distribution Lists and click ‘Update’ button. Now on Exchange Hosting Mode screen user will see that the quota limit is 20 now:

For domains, user will not need to manually update quotas as they will be updated automatically after admin or reseller makes changes in the hosting plan.
