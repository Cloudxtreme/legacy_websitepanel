WebsitePanel Exchange 2007 and Exchange 2010 Modules

Translations:

This knowledge space contains guides, HOW-TOs, tips and tricks for WebsitePanel Exchange 2007 and Exchange 2010 Modules (also referred as "WebsitePanel Hosted Exchange Solution" or "WSP HES" in the articles below) that work with Exchange 2007 and Exchange 2010 RTM.

Please note that the recommended version of Exchange to work with WebsitePanel is Exchange 2010 SP1 with /hosting switch that can be managed with Exchange 2010 SP1 Hosting Mode module. For additional info, pleaser read this support statement.
