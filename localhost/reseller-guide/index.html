WebsitePanel Reseller Guide

Translations:

  • tr-TR

WebsitePanel “Reseller” role is in the middle of roles hierarchy. Reseller account can be created by Administrator or another Reseller. “Reseller” role includes all the functionality of “User” role plus resellers can create hosting plans, customer accounts and then hosting spaces based on hosting plans. Resellers are allowed to create customer accounts of two types: User and Reseller.
